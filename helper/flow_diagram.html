Flow diagram

Maharashtra Teacher Eligibility Test (MAHATET)


1) Visit the website MAHATET site

2) Read all the information related to MAHATET examination on the website, Government decisions and circulars, Exam plan and syllabus, schedule, terms and conditions for the examination, required certificates,other information.

3) After viewing all the information, just click on the "New Registration" button on the homepage.

4) After reading the registration instructions carefully and clicking on the checkbox at the bottom of the instructions, just click on the "New Registration" button.

5) Fill in the information displayed in the online registration application opened first name, father's name, last name, date of birth (like SSC certificate), gender, mobile number and email id.

6) If you want to make some changes to the form, you can make changes using the Upodate Information. Click on update Information link.

7) Give the Test neat and Properly.

8) Mkae sure that Certificate is available for Download after the Exam.
