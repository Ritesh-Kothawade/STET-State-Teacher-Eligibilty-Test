Maharashtra STET Eligibility Details: Maharashtra Teacher Eligibility Test (MAHATET) Eligibility details are mentioned below…

I. Nationality: Candidate must be a citizen of Maharashtra.

Teacher Eligibility Test – Maharashtra (MAHA-TET) Levels as Follows:

A. I will be for a person who intends to be a teacher for classes 1st-5th (Lower Primary Level).
B. I will be for a person who intends to be a teacher for classes 6th-10th (Upper Primary Level).
C. I will be for a person who intends to be a teacher for classes 11th-12th (Secondary Level)

II. Eligibility:

The following persons shall be eligible for appearing in the TET:

i. A person who has acquired the academic and professional qualifications specified in the Government of Maharashtra Resolution.

ii. A person who is pursuing any of the teacher education courses (recognized by the appropriate authority, as the case may be) specified in the Government of Maharashtra Resolution.

III. Frequency and Validity:

  • The Government of Maharashtra will conduct a TET at least once every year.
  • The Validity Period of TET qualifying certificate is seven years for all categories form the date of award of certificate.
